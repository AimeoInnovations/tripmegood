Why Personalized Travel Planning

Travel as you are

We all have different expectations and aspirations when we travel so why should the experience be the same? Choose the trip that suits your personality and interests and not what the travel agents sells.

Flexible Trips

Flexibility with everything right from choosing the dates to the activities you want to do on the trip. Travel off-season if you want to or do a last minute booking when you are absolutely sure - all this with personalized trips.

Travel in your Budget

Control the trip cost by transparent pricing and flexible planning. Know what your paying for and choose to add or drop a thing or two if needed.

“à la carte” Travel

Customize everything from Hotels to Taxis, Activities to F&B on a need basis. Its like building an itinerary a-la-carte.

All Inclusive Trips

Do not leave anything to chance but pre-negotiate the prices for your whole trip and add them to the inclusions - No "fine print". That ways there are no surprises. No cash or card hassles. Choose to pay in advance or installments without having to carry cash on your trip.

One stop shop

Our travel experts have a list of destinations, activities in them, accommodations and well negotiated prices on almost all travel needs. So look nowhere else and have your trip well planned at a single point.
