Our Journey

A cramped college dorm room, late-night conversations, and a flicker of inspiration. That’s where our story began—a humble startup fueled by passion and wanderlust. As the years unfolded, so did our vision. We transformed from a scrappy startup into a full-service boutique travel planner. Our passport-stamped journey now spans more than 60 countries. Whether it’s sipping espresso in Rome, chasing waterfalls in Bali, or marveling at the Northern Lights in Iceland, we’ve got you covered. Today, we’ve had the privilege of serving over 4,000 customers across the globe. From bustling cities to serene landscapes, our travelers have explored every corner of our beautiful planet.

Behind every great adventure lies a passionate soul. Our founder, with over 12 years of experience in the travel technology domain, has woven his expertise into our company’s fabric. He has built travel products from scratch, across product, supply and demand collaborating with industry giants like OYO, Hellotravel (by Indiamart), Thomas Cook, Cox & Kings, Ebix, and more.

What truly sets us apart? It’s our unwavering commitment to creating unforgettable memories. We are a close-knit team that takes on a limited number of clients and ensures each of their trips is executed close to perfection. You’ll often find our founder personally engaging with customers chatting about hidden gems, sharing insider tips, crafting bespoke itineraries and recommending that cozy café in Paris. Why? Because we believe that travel isn’t just about ticking off destinations; it’s about weaving stories and forging connections.

So, whether you’re a couple celebrating love or a family seeking adventure, we pour our hearts into every trip. So, when you embark on a journey with us, know that you’re not just a client—you’re part of our travel family.
