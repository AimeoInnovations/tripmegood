Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

It's simple. You create content about your travel experiences, we connect travelers to unforgettable experiences. For each successful booking, you'll make a commission.

Is there a cost to join?

No, it's completely free to join. There are no monthly or annual fees.

How do I sign up?

Just fill out the above form and we'll be in touch.

What type of content can I create?

You can create any type of content that features travel experiences, destinations, or both. This can include blog posts, videos, photos, and more.

How will I be paid?

You'll be paid through PayPal, UPI, PayTM or Bank transfer.

When will I be paid?

You'll be paid within 30 days of the traveler's check-in date.

How much can I earn?

There is no limit to how much you can earn. You'll receive a revenue share for each successful booking that is made.
